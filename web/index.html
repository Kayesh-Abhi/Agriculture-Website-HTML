Grow , Share and Sell

Get more fresh air

Ever wonder why relaxation music features sounds from nature? Theres something soothing and tranquil about connecting with the living world around us.

Most of us forget that there’s nature growing in our own backyard. Head outdoors and plant some veggies - you'll be surprised how good you feel.

Better for you

Pressure is mounting on big agriculture to produce more and more per acre, leading to widespread use of artificial fertilizers, chemicals, pesticides and now genetically modified foods.
In your garden, you decide: allow nature to work it’s magic, bugs and all; use organic pest control methods, such as netting and companion planting; or employ the help of chemicals.

Growing your own gives you more control over what goes in your mouth.

Enjoy fruitful hobby

Growing your own food is incredibly rewarding.

You know what we mean if you’ve ever eaten those first juicy, sweet fruits off your own tree. When all the work of planting and watering culminates in that burst of sugar on your tongue, and the plump flesh oozes juice down your wrists as you munch away.

And sharing your food with others takes your pleasure to a whole new level!

Earn extra money

Growing and selling home grown produce is a great way to make more money!

Contribute and Socialise

With modern technology and a hectic work life we’ve slowly lost touch with the simple joys of contributing to make our world and local communities a better place.

Don't let your excess crops go waste

Got that crazy tree bursting with so much fruit the branches touch the ground? How about those veggies that have gone wild all over your backyard? Ever said “There’s no way we can eat all that!”?

Don’t let that food go to waste! Share it with the world.
